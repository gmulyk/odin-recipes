Brownies

You likely already have the ingredients you'll need for these easy brownies on hand!

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups of white sugar
  • flour
  • butter
  • eggs
  • cocoa powder
  • vanilla extract
  • baking powder
  • salt
  • walnuts

Steps:

  1. Mix wet ingredients together in mixing bowl
  2. Mix wet ingredients together in separate bowl
  3. Slowly combine dry and wet ingredients, while stirring
  4. Bake in preheated oven until top is dry
  5. Cool, then cut into squares
