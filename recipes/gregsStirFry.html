Greg's Peanut Chicken Stir Fry

A quick and simple dinner to prepare on lazy nights, but delicious enough for fancy dinner parties as well!

Ingredients:

  • Shredded pieces of BBQ chicken, or precooked slivers of chicken breast
  • broccoli
  • onion
  • coconut oil OR butter
  • peanut butter
  • lemon
  • Spices: chili powder, cayenne, curry powder, garlic, oregano, basil
  • soy sauce
  • honey

Steps:

  1. Saute onions in butter or coconut oil in frypan
  2. Add chicken and veggies, and mix around to coat in oil
  3. Add peanut butter and honey, mix as they melt around the chicken and veggies
  4. Sprinkle lemon juice, spices, and soy sauce to taste
  5. Add water to pan if sauce is too thick
  6. Serve over rice!
